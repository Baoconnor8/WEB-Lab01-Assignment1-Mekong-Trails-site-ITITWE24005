🇻🇳 Vietnam 7 Days Culture + Nature Budget-Friendly

Vietnam Highlights: Hanoi → Ha Long → Hội An

The classic north-to-south route condensed into 7 unforgettable days. Perfect for first-time visitors.

  1. Day 1–2 · Hanoi: Old Quarter street food tour, Hoan Kiem Lake, Temple of Literature.
  2. Day 3–4 · Ha Long Bay: Overnight junk cruise, kayaking through limestone caves.
  3. Day 5 · Đà Nẵng: Fly south, check into a beachfront guesthouse, evening at Han Market.
  4. Day 6–7 · Hội An: Ancient Town walking tour, tailor shop, An Bàng Beach, Lantern Festival.
Book This Route Est. budget: US$400–700 excl. flights
🌏 Multi-country 14 Days Culture + Temples Mid-Range

Grand Indochina Loop: Vietnam → Cambodia → Laos

Three countries, two river crossings, one slow boat — this is Southeast Asia at its most epic.

  1. Day 1–3 · Ho Chi Minh City: War Remnants Museum, Ben Thanh Market, Mekong Delta day trip.
  2. Day 4–6 · Siem Reap: Angkor Archaeological Park 3-day temple pass.
  3. Day 7 · Phnom Penh: Royal Palace, Tuol Sleng Genocide Museum.
  4. Day 8–10 · 4000 Islands (Si Phan Don): Hammock time, Irrawaddy dolphins, Khone Falls.
  5. Day 11–14 · Luang Prabang: Slow boat on the Mekong, Alms-giving ceremony, Kuang Si Falls.
Book This Route Est. budget: US$900–1,500 excl. flights
🇻🇳 Vietnam 10 Days Trekking + Culture Adventure

Northern Highlands Trek: Hanoi → Ninh Bình → Sapa → Hà Giang

Deep into Vietnam's spectacular north — rice terraces, karst mountains, and village homestays.

  1. Day 1–2 · Hanoi: Arrival, West Lake cafés, depart on overnight train to Ninh Bình.
  2. Day 3 · Ninh Bình: Boat through Tràng An limestone landscape (UNESCO).
  3. Day 4–6 · Sapa: Guided trek to Cát Cát and Y Tý villages, Hmong homestay.
  4. Day 7–10 · Hà Giang Loop: 300km motorbike loop through the Đồng Văn Karst Plateau — Vietnam's most breathtaking road.
Book This Route Est. budget: US$600–900 excl. flights
