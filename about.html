Our Story

Born on a slow boat along the Mekong, fuelled by strong coffee and curiosity.

How It Started

A Slow Boat and a Big Idea

In 2019, two backpackers — Ana and Minh — spent three days on a slow boat from Huay Xai to Luang Prabang. No Wi-Fi, no agenda. Just river, jungle, and conversation with fellow travellers who all asked the same question: "Where should I go next?"

They launched Mekong Trails as a simple blog. Today it's a full team of seven writers, photographers, and local guides, covering Vietnam, Cambodia, Laos, Thailand, and Myanmar with obsessive detail.

Our golden rule: we never accept payment for positive coverage. Every recommendation is one we'd give to a close friend.
