Privacy Policy & Credits

Last updated: September 2025

Policy content

1. Introduction

Mekong Trails ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights. By using our website, you agree to these terms.

2. Data We Collect

When you submit a trip request form or subscribe to our newsletter, we may collect:

  • Full name and email address
  • Phone number and travel dates
  • Travel preferences and budget information
  • Any special requests or uploaded files you provide

We also collect anonymised analytics data (page views, country of origin) via privacy-friendly tools to improve our content.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To build and send your personalised travel itinerary
  • To follow up about your travel enquiry via email or phone
  • To send our newsletter (only if you opted in)
  • To improve our website and content

We never sell your data to third parties. We never use your information for paid advertising targeting.

4. Data Retention

Trip request data is retained for 24 months and then permanently deleted unless you have an active account. Newsletter subscribers may unsubscribe at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email.

5. Cookies

This site uses minimal, privacy-respecting cookies for session management and basic analytics only. No third-party tracking or advertising cookies are set.

6. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection law, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, email us at privacy@mekongtrails.travel.

7. External Links

Our website links to third-party services including Google Maps, YouTube (Google LLC), and Unsplash. These services have their own privacy policies which we encourage you to review.

8. Contact

For privacy enquiries: privacy@mekongtrails.travel
